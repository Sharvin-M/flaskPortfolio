Data Analysis

How do different factors contribute to a student's performance on an exam?

I used a dataset showcasing different factors and their relation to a students performance on 3 sections of an exam(reading, writing, and math). The research framework I developed focused on answering the overarching question: What factor from the dataset has the greatest impact on a students performance in any given section of the exam. To do so, I formed groups using the different factors and scores from the exams and compared them to find which had the highest correlation. The results of my study concluded that parental level of education was the factor that most greatly affected the performance of a given student on all 3 portions of an exam. Github
